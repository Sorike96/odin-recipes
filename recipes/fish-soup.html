Fish Soup

Desrciption

Traditional hungarian dish, the Fish Soup.

Ingredients

  • 3 kg fish
  • 3 big head onions
  • 3 spoon paprika(sweet grounded)
  • 3,6 l water
  • salt
  • 500 g pasta

Steps

  1. Clean the fish and set aside the fish sauce and milk. Grind, slice, then add salt to taste. Set aside for 1 hour.
  2. Clean the red onions and cut them into small cubes.
  3. At the end of the hour, put the fish slices and the onion in the pot first, then pour 3.6 l of water.
  4. Boil the juice. When the foam has boiled off, sprinkle the paprika.
  5. After 15-20 minutes, add the giblets and the hot cherry pepper, then you need another 20 minutes of cooking.It will be good if the paprika does not stick out on the edge of the spoon when tasting.
  6. Meanwhile, prepare the pasta in a separate pot.
